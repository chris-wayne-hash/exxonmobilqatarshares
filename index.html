About ExxonMobil Qatar

Leading Qatar's Energy Sector for Over Two Decades

ExxonMobil Qatar has been at the forefront of the nation's oil and gas industry since 1998, driving innovation and sustainable development in one of the world's most important energy regions.

Our vision is to provide reliable energy solutions while maintaining the highest standards of safety, environmental responsibility, and community engagement. We are committed to supporting Qatar's National Vision 2030 through strategic investments and technological advancements.

With a dedicated team of over 5,000 professionals, ExxonMobil Qatar continues to expand its operations, strengthen partnerships, and contribute to the nation's economic prosperity.
